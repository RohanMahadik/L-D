Selection process

Welcome to Union Advith!

Step 1: Check if you are eligible for applying for Union Advith if you pass the following criteria:

  • Minimum tenure completed service with the Bank should be 5 years for Scale IV, 7 years for Scale V & 10 years for Scale VI executives
  • Residual services of minimum 10 years for Scale IV, 7 years for Scale V & 5 years for Scale VI executives
  • An average annual EKAM score of at least 75 in the past 2 years (when the score is available)
  • 100% completion of mandatory E-learning modules in the past 2 years
  • Must not be on deputation to any other organization
  • Must not have an on-going (active) vigilance or non-vigilance cases at any stage

Step 2: Congrats if you are eligible for Unionâ€™s Advith! As a next step, kindly fill the Application Form which is accessible on the EKAM portal through the Union Advith microsite. Ensure to diligently fill up the answers to the questions and reserve at least 30 minutes to 1 hour of your time for filling up the form.

Step 3: While submitting the Application Form, please ensure that you cross check if the external awards and certifications visible on the Parivar portal are correct. In case of any discrepancy(s), kindly update the details on the Parivar portal on priority since these details are also used in the selection process.

Step 4: Once you have submitted the application form and updated your awards & certification details, you are almost there! Kindly nudge all your managers (in the past 5 years) to fill up your recommendation form, since it constitutes a big part of the final score used for selection as well.
