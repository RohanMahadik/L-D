About LDP

The Leadership Development Program stands out as a premier initiative designed to cultivate the visionary leaders of tomorrow. Tailored specifically for middle management, this unique program offers a comprehensive approach to leadership growth through a series of in-depth assessments, including behavioural skills, technical and functional skills, and 360-degree feedback. The program prioritizes personalized development by providing 1-on-1 coaching sessions to address specific challenges identified during assessments. The extensive curriculum spans a wide range of topics, covering behavioural competencies such as decision-making and conflict resolution, functional expertise in banking operations, sales, compliance, and digital trends, as well as new-age skills like advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles. Participants will benefit from numerous hours of in-person training, conducted across various domains to ensure a well-rounded skill set. The program also includes practical capstone projects, allowing participants to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios. Ultimately, we measure the program's success through a thorough impact assessment, ensuring that our leaders are not only prepared for today's challenges but are also equipped to drive future innovations and success.

Mission

Empowering leadership excellence, dedicated to cultivating visionary leaders who drive innovation and adaptability

Vision

Our mission at Union Drishti is to cultivate a highly skilled leadership pool, driving innovation and organizational change, positioning Union Bank as the premier industry experience. Through fostering a startup environment and functioning as an internal think-tank, we aim to pivot our L&D unit into a key revenue center.
