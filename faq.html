Leadership Development Program

The mission of the program is to empower leadership excellence, dedicated to cultivating visionary leaders who drive innovation and adaptability.

The program is open to employees who fit into the eligibility criteria as per the given Selection Process. The aim is to provide this exclusive opportunity to employees having great leadership potential and a commitment to personal and professional growth.

Interested candidates can apply through the Application Form available to you. Make sure you fit into the eligible criteria before you fill up the form.

The program lasts for one year, with training sessions held every two months, coaching sessions spread across and other learning interventions as well.

The program includes in-person trainings, workshops, one-on-one coaching sessions, group projects, and case studies.

In addition to receiving the coveted completion certificate of Union Advith, employees will gain advanced leadership skills, increased visibility within the organization, potential for career advancement and opportunities to network with like-minded peers.

Applying to LDP

You can apply for the program through the application form available on the EKAM portal through the Union’s Advith microsite. It is mandatory for all eligible employees to apply for the program.

You are advised to spend at least 30 mins to 1 hour for filling the form. We encourage you to not wait till the deadline to start filling the form, kindly plan to take out time well in advance and complete it.

The deadline for filling the form is 19 June 2024.

You are required to login since we need to capture Employee name and PF ID along with the other details submitted. It is a measure to ensure that only eligible employees are able to fill the form.

You need to ensure that your awards and certifications displayed on the portal are up-to-date and accurate. In case those are not updated, please update the same by 17 June’24 on the Parivar portal. You also need to nudge and ensure that your past and current managers fill up their recommendation inputs for you.

Eligibility Criteria

• Scale IV executives: Minimum 5 years of service in the bank • Scale V executives: Minimum 7 years of service in the bank • Scale VI executives: Minimum 10 years of service in the bank This calculation would be made as of 31st March 2024.

• Scale IV executives: At least 10 years of residual service • Scale V executives: At least 7 years of residual service • Scale VI executives: At least 5 years of residual service This calculation would be made as of 31st March 2024.

Residual service is calculated based on the remaining years an executive is expected to serve with the bank before their superannuation. The cutoff date for the same shall be 31st March 2024.

Executives must have an average annual EKAM score of at least 75 in the past 2 years to be eligible for the program.

Executives whose EKAM marks are not available for the last two years due to sabbatical leave or authorized leave on medical grounds will be considered based on the appraisal marks from the last two available years.

Yes, there are 3 more conditions apart from the minimum years of service, residual years of service and average appraisal marks. - 100% completion of mandatory E-learning modules in the past 2 years - Must not be on deputation to any other organization Must not have an ongoing (active) vigilance or non-vigilance case

Eligible executives must apply by submitting an application form that includes personal details, a self-declaration, and responses to evaluative leadership-related behavioral questions. The application form will be digitally accessible.

Selection Criteria

- Average EKAM Percentile Score of last 2 years: 30% - Application Form: 25% - Manager Recommendations: 25% - Special Achievements - Certifications: 5% - Management Inputs: 15%

A total of 600 participants will be selected each year, comprising 450 frontline leaders (Scale IV) and 150 emerging leaders (Scale V & VI).

The EKAM cohort percentile score is derived from the averaged cohort-specific percentile scores spanning FY 2022-23 and FY 2023-24. The scores are mapped to a score out of 30, with the highest percentile receiving the full 30 marks and lower percentiles scaled accordingly.

Manager recommendations are collected through a structured digital feedback form. Managers rate the employee on various competency areas using a scale from 1 to 5. Feedback from the last three managers (or a minimum of two) in scales higher than the applicant’s present scale will be considered.

Certifications from both foreign/domestic universities and online platforms are considered. The scoring is based on the number of certifications and the credentials of the issuing institutions, with a maximum of 5 points available.

Management inputs are collected from key organizational figures to provide high-level perspectives and insights into the participant's potential, contributing significantly to the overall assessment.

The selection process is strategically designed to ensure a diverse mix of participants by considering various job scales, current postings, job families, and gender representation. This approach helps maintain a balanced and inclusive group of leaders.

Conclaves and Coaching Sessions

The coaching sessions will each last around one hour, which will largely depend on the coach and coachee’s conversation. During these sessions, conversations with expert coaches, who are specifically trained in leadership development, will take place. Personalized feedback, guidance, and specific techniques and training methods aimed at enhancing various aspects of personality and leadership skills will be provided.

No, specific preparation is not required for the coaching sessions. You are only required to be fully aware of the assessment reports and 360 evaluation report.

The coaches are experienced professionals who have spent years conducting sessions for leadership development. Your assigned coach would have an in-depth understanding of various personality aspects and would be well-versed in different coaching methods to meet your individual needs.

It will be a 9-day residential program conducted in three conclaves. Each conclave will last for three days and will be held in either Bangalore, Hyderabad, or Gurgaon.

Industry veterans with extensive experience in the banking sector, reputed leadership trainers, and academicians from prestigious institutions, who are experts in their fields and well-versed in interactive teaching methods, will be involved.

The program will cover a range of topics, including behavioral subjects such as decision-making and mindfulness. It will also address new age topics like design thinking, artificial intelligence, and data analytics. Additionally, there would also be sessions on functional areas such as collections and recovery, sales and marketing, operations, HR and digital banking.
